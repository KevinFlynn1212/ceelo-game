📜 House Rules
🎲 The Game

Cee-Lo is a 2–5 player dice game. Each player rolls 3 dice trying to beat the other players. Highest hand wins the pot.

🏆 Hand Rankings (High → Low)
1.🌟 Strungflowers (4-5-6) — Instant win. Unbeatable.
2.⚡ Trips (Three of a Kind) — Three matching dice. Higher trips beats lower (e.g. Trips 6 beats Trips 3).
3.🎯 Point (Pair + Any) — A pair plus a third die. The third die (1–6) is your point. Higher point wins. Same point = Shootout.
✗🐉 Dancing Dragon (1-2-3) — Instant loss.
✗Bust — No combo after 7 attempts. Automatic loss.
—No Combo — No pair, no special — re-roll.
💰 Antes
  • Each round starts with everyone paying the ante (the alley's stake level).
  • Antes are collected automatically when the round begins.
  • Can't afford the ante? You'll be removed from the table and can rebuy.
⚔️ Shootout (Tiebreaker)

If two or more players finish with the same point value, they enter a Shootout. Tied players re-roll until one wins. Optional raise window opens before each shootout roll.

🐉 All Dancing Dragon

If every player rolls Dancing Dragon (1-2-3), nobody wins. The pot doubles and carries over to the next round.

🏦 House Rake

The house takes a 5% rake from each winning pot. Displayed as a deduction when you win.

⏱ Timers
  • 10 seconds to roll each turn — miss it and you're auto-rolled.
  • 5 seconds between rounds — tap Play Again to stay in or you sit out.
  • Miss 5 rounds in a row and you're removed from the table.
💸 Buy-In & Rebuys

Every player starts with $500. If you go broke, you can rebuy back to $500 at any time from the lobby.
